About me

I'm a web designer with more than 2 years of amateur experience. If you're looking for animations or "wow effects", you'd better change direction! In my works I always try to combine aesthetic with functional aspects, looking for the right balance to communicate without annoying. What makes a great website? Here's my opinion: a successful website should be attractive but easy to read. A fascinating message, together with an immediate understanding. Original content and easily traceable information: that's my recipe!
